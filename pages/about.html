Megalo Empire Animal Clinic - Providing Expert Care, Even on Weekends

Directed by Dr. Haniza, who has been actively involved in veterinary medicine since August 2017, the clinic is dedicated to providing high-quality care for animals. Dr. Haniza began her career at the Batu Pahat, Johor branch of the same organization before relocating to Bintulu. She specializes in soft tissue surgery and holds a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) degree from Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM) Serdang, where she also completed her foundation studies. With over six years of professional experience, she is committed to ensuring the well-being of animals through expert medical treatment and compassionate service.
